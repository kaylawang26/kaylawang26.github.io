Get Involved

Volunteer Opportunities

Crossing Guard

Assist with pedestrian traffic at major intersections within the festival area. You will be provided with a safety vest and flag and briefed on how to signal for pedestrian traffic. This is an outdoor standing position.

Grounds Maintenance

Monitor area for loose trash and any spills or broken glass, empty trash receptacles as needed.

Vendor Relief

Check in with vendors and provide supervision of their booth as needed so they can take breaks for food, restroom use, etc

Hospitality Ambassador

Provide information and answer questions at the Information booth or around the festival, check in volunteers at the info tent.

Family Fun Zone Assistant

Supervise games at the Family Fun Zone in Dewitt Park for children and families, monitor for safe behavior and answer questions

Source: Sign Up Genius from the Downtown Ithaca Alliance
