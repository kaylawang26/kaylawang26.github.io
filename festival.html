Gift Basket Contest

The Apple Harvest Festival is hosting a gift basket contest using donated items from downtown stores. To enter, go on the posted photo of the gift basket on the Apple Harvest Facebook Event page. The question "What is Your Favorite Thing about our Apple Harvest Festival?" will be posted alongside it.

Anyone who offers a response to the post question will be automatically entered to win the gift basket contest. On Thursday, Oct. 29, 2022, we'll randomly pull the name of a winner from those who commented on the post. This winner will be notified via Facebook that they need to pick up the prize at the Apple Harvest Festival information tent during the event!

Source: Downtown Ithaca Website
